Getting Started

Use the tabs at the top to configure the program.

This program will build you a custom Planner, tailored to your class schedule and the school year!

Use the Settings tab to configure the output and the block schedule.

Use the Schedule tab to input your class schedule.

Use the Calendar tab to update any snow days or interruptions in the block schedule.

The resulting planner will appear under the Planner tab.

Printing

The planners are printable!

The program will do its best to make sure each month fits on its own page, however it might not always work things out perfectly.

Be sure to use print preview. If your browser has a shrink to fit option, enable it, and make sure to also enable print backgrounds if you want to see any shading or colors.

Some browsers let you disable the headers and footers. It is also recommended that you reduce the margins as much as possible.

If shrink to fit fails to fit each month onto its own page, try adjusting the scale until it does fit.

Each tab is printable, so be sure to select the planner tab if you want to print your year's calendar. This program could also be used to print your block schedule or a one page calendar.

For best results, use Firefox or Chrome on PC/Windows and Safari or Chrome on Mac.

Class Schedule

Fill out your class schedule. To begin, enter all your classes into the class list by hitting the big "+" sign.

Once all your classes are entered, enter your schedule by clicking in each cell. When you click, it will cycle through each one of your classes.

Finally, if your schedule looks correct, you should proceed to the Planner tab!

You do not need to enter in every section if you do not want it displayed on the planner.

(By default, there are seven periods so you can fit your lunch onto the schedule. If you don't put your lunch, just ignore the seventh period.)

You may type a background color for your class.
